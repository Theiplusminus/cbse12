How to Prepare for CBSE 12th Boards

Start early, stay consistent, and focus on NCERT books. Practice sample papers and previous year questions to familiarize yourself with the exam pattern.

  • Revise regularly and maintain a study schedule.
  • Focus on key subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Math.
  • Take short breaks to stay energized.
